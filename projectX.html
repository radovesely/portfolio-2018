TASK: Visual identity, concept development , website

Student-driven creative studio offering unique digital solutions for companies.

Story
Founded in 2018, our mission is to build a creative studio which will bring passionate students, who are eager to put their theory into practise and like-minded companies under one roof. We believe this new way of cooperation between students and companies would bring many exceptional ideas to life.

MISSION: to help students get hands-on real-life work experience and improve their theoretical knowledge.

To communicate our mission right, there was a need to create appealing visual identity to catch the attention of the target audience - young creative professional and companies to connect the students with the real life experience.

The design is inspired by students scriblling in their notebooks while being in the class. Youth and creativity is the key of the design with a touch of imperfections which are representing growing and improving.
