Folkekirkens nødhjælp / DanChurchAid

DanChurchAid assists the worlds poorest to lead a life in dignity. Aid is given regardless of race, creed, political or religious affiliation.

DanChurchAid is based in Denmark and a member of the Act Alliance together with 110 other organizations.

DanChurchAid assisted 3,6 million people trough humanitarian and development activities in 29 countries during 2016.
