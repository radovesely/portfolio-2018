Project Zabka

TASK: Visual identity, concept development , website , business strategy

COLLABORATORS ON THIS PROJECT:
Tomas Gazi, Margarita Avin, Jevgenia Artjuhhina

This project was part of my 2nd semester exam where we had to develop the online presence of the chosen artist

Andrea Klváčková, also known as a ZABKA is an artist who is transforming ordinary white sneakers into beautiful artworks.

We started by brainstorming our concepts, and continuing by developing our ideas. Part of the project was to create a full visual identity with the proper logo, business plan, video content for SoMe and CMS with full explanation for the artist how to use the website.

“BRING YOUR SNEAKERS TO LIFE” The slogan what represents the artist’s work and very personal approach.

Examples of the logo.

The website was built very carefully with strong focus on artist needs and style. Firstly we did the wireframes and later tested them. Next step was to create a visually appealing user interface and test the design as well. We were inspired by artist’s personal style of designing sneakers and bringing them into life.
